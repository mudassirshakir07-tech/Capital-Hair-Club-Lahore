The studio

A saloon chair and a classroom seat, under one roof

Mirha Beauty Saloon & Training Centre was built on a simple belief: beauty work is a craft, and craft deserves both an artist's hand and a teacher's patience. Our chairs host brides, everyday clients and quinceañera glow-ups; our classroom hosts the next generation of makeup artists, hairstylists and skin specialists.

Every service is performed with premium, skin-friendly products, and every course is taught hands-on — no crowded lecture halls, just real faces, real techniques, real confidence.
